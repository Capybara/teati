Tea Timer iOS App

TeaTi

  • Select from Black, Green, or White teas.
  • Then press start to begin the brew timer.
  • To set custom timers for your tea, press "settings" in the upper right hand corner of the main screen.
  • Move slider knobs to desired brew time.
  • When finished press "back".

For TeaTi support, email teati@downtowndistro.com
