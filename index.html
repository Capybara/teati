Tea Ti

Tea timer for iPhone®


    Select from Black, Green, or White teas.

  • Press start to begin the brew timer
  • Press and hold start to reset time

    To set custom timers for your tea:

  • Press "Settings" in the upper right hand corner of the main screen
    Note: Swipe up to hide the "Settings" toolbar / Swipe down to reveal the "Settings" toolbar
  • Move slider knobs to the desired brew time
  • Press "Back" in the upper left hand corner of the "Settings" screen.

    To restore original brew times:

  • Press "Restore" in the upper right hand corner of the "Settings" screen.
